## Current product status

**Organisation-level verified domains** (prove you control `@company.com` to
unlock domain-gated invites, SSO-style assumptions, or automatic membership
rules) are **not documented as a shipped feature** in the open-source ctx|
stack at this time.

Today, access is governed by **explicit invitations** and **roles** — see
[Adding team members](/docs/managing-organisation/team-members).

## What you can do today

- **Invite colleagues by email** from **Settings → Members**.
- **Use social login** (GitHub / Google / Microsoft) where enabled on your
  deployment — still subject to invite + membership for a given org.
- **Self-host** and keep identity entirely in infrastructure you control; see
  [Authentication](/docs/self-hosting/authentication).

## Why this page exists

Enterprise buyers often ask for **domain verification** early. This doc sets
expectations: ctx| is honest about what is wired today so you do not plan an
onboarding flow that the OSS UI cannot yet enforce.

If domain verification is critical for your rollout, track product updates on
[ctxpipe.ai](https://ctxpipe.ai) or discuss with the team via
[Get in touch](/docs/getting-started/get-in-touch).
