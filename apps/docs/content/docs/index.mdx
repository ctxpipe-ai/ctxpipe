## What is ctx?

ctx is a self-learning knowledge graph infrastructure for autonomous coding agents.
It gives agents the context they need to understand your architecture, follow your
standards, and build on what your team has already learned.

## How it works

1. **Connect your repositories** — Point ctx at your repos and monorepos. Git is
   the source of truth.
2. **The system indexes and learns** — The knowledge graph ingests your codebase,
   dependencies, ADRs, and agent interactions.
3. **Connect your agent via MCP** — One connection gives any agent full graph
   access during planning and execution.
